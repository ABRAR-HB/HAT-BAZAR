# Chapter 2: Non-Negotiable Business Rules

The following business rules are Final Decisions of the HAT-BAZAR project.

These rules cannot be modified unless the Project Owner officially approves a new version of this Constitution.

---

# Seller Rules

- One Seller Account can manage multiple Shops.
- Every new Shop requires a separate Shop Registration Fee.
- Every Shop must have a unique Shop Name.
- Every Shop has its own Products, Orders, Dashboard, Ratings and Business Identity.
- A Seller may switch between owned Shops from a single Seller Account.

---

# Shop Verification Rules

- AI verifies only Shop Logo and Shop Name.
- AI cannot permanently approve or reject a Shop.
- Suspicious Shops are forwarded to Admin Review.
- Final authority always belongs to Admin.

---

# Product Rules

- Every Product must contain at least one Image.
- Product Video is supported.
- Product Stock can never become negative.
- Product Stock is managed automatically by the system.
- AI may review Product Media and suspicious Products.
- AI cannot permanently delete any Product.

---

# Order Rules

- Every Order has a unique Order ID.
- Orders follow the official HAT-BAZAR Order Workflow.
- Order History is permanent.
- Successfully completed Orders can never be deleted.

---

# Rider & Delivery Rules

- All Deliveries are performed only by HAT-BAZAR Riders.
- Sellers cannot use their own Delivery Service.
- Customers cannot choose Riders.
- Rider Assignment is controlled only by the HAT-BAZAR System.
- Customer Cancellation is allowed only before Rider Pickup starts.
- After Pickup starts, cancellation follows HAT-BAZAR Policy.
- Delivery is completed only after successful Delivery Confirmation.

---

# Delivery Proof Rules

Product Pickup requires Seller Confirmation.

Product Delivery requires Customer Confirmation.

Delivery is considered completed only after:

- Seller Signature during Pickup
- Customer Signature during Delivery
- Delivery Receipt Completion

Delivery Proof must be permanently stored for verification and dispute resolution.

---

# Chat Rules

- Customer always starts the first conversation.
- Seller cannot initiate Promotional Chats.
- Chat History is permanently stored.
- AI never sends Messages automatically.

---

# Notification Rules

- Important Notifications are mandatory.
- Security Alerts cannot be disabled.
- Emergency Notices from Admin cannot be disabled.

---

# Badge System Rules

- HAT-BAZAR uses a 15-Level Badge System.
- Every new Seller starts from Level 4.
- Badge Levels must always progress sequentially.
- Badge Levels cannot be skipped.
- Badge Purchase also follows sequential progression.
- Badge Purchase is allowed only up to Level 7 (Gold).
- Levels 8–15 can only be earned through Business Performance.
- Poor Business Performance or Policy Violations may reduce Badge Level.
- Monthly Charge is determined according to Badge Level.
- If a Seller falls below Level 4, the Monthly Charge remains equal to the Level 4 Charge.
- Higher Badge Levels provide higher Business Benefits.
- Delivery Performance does not affect Seller Badge because Delivery is managed by HAT-BAZAR.

---

# AI Rules

AI acts only as an Assistant.

AI cannot make final Business Decisions.

AI cannot permanently suspend:

- Seller
- Customer
- Rider
- Shop
- Product

Final authority always belongs to Admin.

---

# Project Governance

If any Blueprint conflicts with this Constitution, MASTER_PROJECT.md always takes priority.

Any exception requires explicit approval from the Project Owner.

# Chapter 7: Marketplace Economy

HAT-BAZAR operates as a managed marketplace where every business activity follows transparent, scalable and sustainable economic principles.

The marketplace is designed to create long-term value for Customers, Sellers, Riders and HAT-BAZAR itself.

---

# Revenue Sources

HAT-BAZAR may generate revenue through:

- Shop Registration Fees
- Monthly Seller Charges
- Badge Upgrade Purchases
- Sales Commission
- Premium Marketplace Services
- Advertising & Promotions
- Future Financial Services

---

# Seller Economy

Every Seller operates under a single Seller Account.

A Seller may own multiple Shops.

Each newly created Shop requires a separate Shop Registration Fee.

Each Shop operates independently while remaining under the same Seller Account.

---

# Badge Economy

Seller reputation is managed through the official Badge System.

Badge Levels influence:

- Marketplace Trust
- Business Visibility
- Premium Features
- Monthly Charges

Badge progression is based primarily on long-term business performance.

---

# Customer Economy

Customers are never charged for creating an account.

Customers pay only for marketplace services they choose to use.

Future premium customer services may be introduced.

---

# Rider Economy

All deliveries are managed by HAT-BAZAR Riders.

Rider compensation is determined by official marketplace policies.

Seller Badge Levels are never affected by Rider performance.

---

# Financial Transparency

Every financial activity must be:

- Traceable
- Auditable
- Secure
- Properly Recorded

Financial records must comply with marketplace policies.

---

# Sustainability

Every economic decision should support:

- Marketplace Growth
- Fair Competition
- Business Stability
- Long-term Scalability

Short-term profit must never compromise marketplace trust.
