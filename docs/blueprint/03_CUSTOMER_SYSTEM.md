# HAT-BAZAR Blueprint

# Customer System

Version: 1.0

Status: Final Blueprint

---

# Purpose

The Customer System defines how Customers interact with the HAT-BAZAR marketplace.

It provides customer profile management, shopping preferences, saved addresses, wishlist, followed shops and activity history while ensuring privacy and security.

---

# Scope

This Blueprint defines:

- Customer Profile
- Address Book
- Wishlist
- Favorite Sellers
- Followed Shops
- Recently Viewed Products
- Customer Preferences
- Account Management
- Business Rules

---

# Core Principles

The Customer System follows these principles:

- Easy Shopping Experience
- Customer Privacy
- Personalization
- Secure Account Management
- AI-assisted Recommendations

---

# Customer Profile

Every Customer Account contains:

- Full Name
- Unique Username
- Mobile Number
- Profile Photo (Optional)
- Registration Date

Customers may update their profile information at any time.

---

# Customer Username

Every Customer has a unique Username.

The Username:

- Must be unique.
- Cannot contain prohibited words.
- May be changed according to marketplace policy.

---

# Address Book

Customers may save unlimited delivery addresses.

Each Address includes:

- Receiver Name
- Mobile Number
- Division
- District
- Upazila/Thana
- Union/Area
- Detailed Address
- GPS Location

One Address may be selected as the Default Address.

---

# Wishlist

Customers may save unlimited Products to their Wishlist.

The Wishlist allows Customers to:

- Save Products for later.
- Remove Products.
- View Product availability.

If a Product returns to stock or its price changes, the Customer may receive a notification.

---

# Follow Shops

Customers may follow unlimited Shops.

Following a Shop allows Customers to receive notifications about:

- New Products
- Offers
- Campaigns
- Live Events
- Important Shop Announcements

---

# General Customer Rules

- Every Customer has a unique Customer ID.
- Customer information is securely stored.
- Customer activity history is permanently recorded.

---

**End of Part 1**


# Recently Viewed Products

The system maintains a history of Products recently viewed by the Customer.

Customers may:

- View Recently Viewed Products.
- Remove individual Products from the history.
- Clear the entire Recently Viewed history.

Recently Viewed Products help improve future AI recommendations.

---

# Favorite Sellers

Customers may save unlimited Sellers as Favorites.

Favorite Sellers appear in a dedicated section of the Customer Dashboard.

Customers may:

- Add Favorite Sellers.
- Remove Favorite Sellers.
- Visit Favorite Seller Shops quickly.

---

# Customer Preferences

Customers may personalize their shopping experience.

Preferences include:

- Preferred Language
- Notification Preferences
- Favorite Categories
- Preferred Delivery Address

Preferences are securely stored and may be updated at any time.

---

# Privacy Settings

Customers control their own privacy settings.

Customers may choose:

- Profile Photo visibility.
- Username visibility.
- Notification preferences.

Sensitive personal information is never publicly displayed.

---

# Account Deactivation

Customers may deactivate their own Accounts.

A deactivated Account:

- Cannot access marketplace services.
- Keeps all Order History.
- Keeps all Payment History.
- Keeps all Audit Records.

Customers may reactivate their Accounts by logging in again.

Permanent Account deletion is not available.

---

# Customer Activity History

The system permanently records Customer activities.

Activity records include:

- Registration
- Login History
- Orders
- Wishlist Activity
- Shop Follow Activity
- Address Changes
- Account Status Changes

Activity records cannot be edited or deleted.

---

# Customer Security

Every Customer Account is protected by:

- OTP Verification
- Secure Authentication
- Login Monitoring
- Suspicious Activity Detection

AI may detect suspicious activities and notify Admin when necessary.

---

**End of Part 2**


# Customer Dashboard

Every Customer has a personalized Dashboard.

The Dashboard displays:

- Profile Information
- Recent Orders
- Wishlist
- Favorite Sellers
- Followed Shops
- Saved Addresses
- Recently Viewed Products
- Account Settings

The Dashboard provides quick access to frequently used marketplace features.

---

# AI Recommendations

The marketplace AI may recommend Products based on:

- Recently Viewed Products
- Wishlist
- Purchase History
- Favorite Categories
- Followed Shops

AI recommendations are designed to improve the Customer shopping experience.

Customers are not required to follow AI recommendations.

---

# Notification Integration

The Customer System integrates with the Notification System.

Customers may receive notifications for:

- Order Updates
- Payment Updates
- Delivery Updates
- Wishlist Price Changes
- Product Back in Stock
- Shop Offers
- Shop Campaigns
- Shop Live Events

Customers may customize notification preferences.

---

# Customer Audit

Every Customer Account maintains an Audit History.

Audit records include:

- Customer ID
- Registration Date
- Login History
- Account Changes
- Address Updates
- Privacy Setting Changes
- Account Status Changes

Audit records are permanent.

---

# Customer Business Rules

- Every Customer has one unique Account.
- Every Customer has one unique Username.
- Customers may save unlimited Addresses.
- One Address may be selected as the Default Address.
- Customers may save unlimited Wishlist Products.
- Customers may follow unlimited Shops.
- Customers may save unlimited Favorite Sellers.
- Customers may deactivate their own Accounts.
- Permanent Account deletion is not available.

---

# Customer Security Policy

The system continuously protects Customer Accounts.

AI may:

- Detect suspicious login activities.
- Detect unusual account behavior.
- Recommend Admin investigation.

AI cannot:

- Suspend Customer Accounts.
- Block Customer Accounts.
- Delete Customer Accounts.

Final authority always belongs to Admin.

Future versions of the Customer System may introduce:

- Family Accounts
- Shared Wishlists
- Gift Registry
- AI Shopping Assistant
- Voice Shopping
- Smart Shopping Preferences

These features require approval from the Project Owner before implementation.

# AI Limitations

AI may:

- Recommend Products.
- Recommend Shops.
- Detect suspicious account activities.
- Improve shopping personalization.
- Recommend Admin Review.

AI cannot:

- Suspend Customer Accounts.
- Block Customer Accounts.
- Delete Customer Accounts.
- Make final enforcement decisions.

Final authority always belongs to Admin.
