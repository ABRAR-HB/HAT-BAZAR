# Purpose

The HAT-BAZAR Badge System is designed to measure Seller reputation, business growth, trust, consistency, and long-term marketplace contribution.

The Badge System motivates Sellers to improve business quality while maintaining fair competition across the marketplace.

---

# Overview

Every Seller is assigned a Badge Level.

The Badge Level represents the Seller's overall business reputation inside HAT-BAZAR.

Higher Badge Levels provide greater recognition, business advantages, and marketplace trust.

---

# Badge Structure

HAT-BAZAR uses a total of **15 Badge Levels**.

Each Badge represents a higher level of business achievement.

Badge Levels always progress sequentially.

No Badge Level may be skipped.

Every newly approved Seller starts from:

**Level 4 — Sapphire**

Levels 1–3 are reserved for downgraded Sellers.

# Badge Upgrade

Badge Upgrade is earned only through long-term business performance.

The system evaluates:

- Business Consistency
- Successful Orders
- Customer Satisfaction
- Policy Compliance
- Business Growth
- Product Quality
- Service Quality

Badge upgrades are intentionally difficult and require sustained performance over time.

---

# Badge Downgrade

A Badge Level may decrease if a Seller:

- Violates Marketplace Policies
- Commits Fraud
- Receives verified serious complaints
- Demonstrates poor long-term business performance

Badge downgrades are reviewed according to HAT-BAZAR policies.

# Badge Purchase

HAT-BAZAR allows limited Badge Purchase.

However, purchased Badges never replace genuine business achievement.

---

# Badge Purchase Rules

Badge Purchase always follows sequential progression.

A Seller cannot skip any Badge Level while purchasing.

Example:

Level 4 → Level 5 → Level 6 → Level 7

Valid

Level 4 → Level 7

Invalid

Level 5 → Level 7

Invalid

---

# Gold Badge

Gold is:

**Level 7**

A Seller may obtain Gold Badge in two ways:

1. Earn through Business Performance

2. Purchase by paying the official Gold Badge Price.

Gold Badge Price is intentionally expensive to maintain marketplace prestige.

---

# Purchase Limitation

Badge Purchase is allowed only up to:

**Level 7 (Gold)**

Levels 8–15 can never be purchased.

They must always be earned.

---

# Higher Badge Levels

Levels 8–15 represent exceptional business reputation.

These Badge Levels require:

- Long-term Business Success
- Excellent Customer Satisfaction
- Marketplace Trust
- Policy Compliance
- Continuous Growth

Money alone can never unlock these Badge Levels.

---

# Monthly Charge

Every Badge Level has its own Monthly Charge.

Higher Badge Levels pay higher Monthly Charges.

However, higher Badge Levels also receive greater business benefits.

If a Seller falls below Level 4,

the Monthly Charge remains equal to the Level 4 Monthly Charge.

---

# Badge Benefits

Higher Badge Levels receive additional marketplace benefits.

Benefits may include:

- Higher Marketplace Visibility
- Better Search Ranking
- Increased Customer Trust
- Premium Marketplace Features
- Exclusive Campaign Eligibility
- Priority Business Support

Benefits are determined by HAT-BAZAR Administration.

---

# Delivery Policy

Seller Badge is never affected by Delivery Performance.

Delivery is the responsibility of HAT-BAZAR.

Seller evaluation considers only Seller-related performance.

Rider performance never decreases Seller Badge Level.

---

# AI Responsibility

AI assists in Badge Evaluation.

AI may recommend:

- Upgrade
- Downgrade
- Review

AI never makes the final decision.

---

# Admin Responsibility

Admin has final authority over:

- Badge Approval
- Badge Downgrade
- Badge Suspension
- Manual Badge Review

All exceptional cases require Admin approval.

---

# Business Rules

- Every Seller has one Badge Level.
- Every new Seller starts from Level 4.
- Badge Levels always progress sequentially.
- Badge Levels cannot be skipped.
- Badge Purchase follows the same progression rules.
- Badge Purchase is limited to Level 7.
- Levels 8–15 are achievement-only.
- Higher Badge Levels receive greater business benefits.
- Monthly Charges increase with Badge Level.
- Badge downgrade is possible for serious policy violations.
- Delivery performance never affects Seller Badge.
