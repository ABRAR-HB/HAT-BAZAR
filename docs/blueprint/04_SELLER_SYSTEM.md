# Seller System

Version: 1.0

Status: Final Blueprint

---

# Purpose

The Seller System defines how Sellers operate their business within the HAT-BAZAR marketplace.

It provides business management tools, seller profile management, shop management, analytics, financial overview and AI-assisted business insights.

---

# Scope

This Blueprint defines:

- Seller Profile
- Seller Dashboard
- Shop Management
- Product Management
- Business Analytics
- Seller Security
- Seller Documents
- Seller Notifications
- Seller Business Rules

---

# Core Principles

The Seller System follows these principles:

- Simple Business Management
- Secure Marketplace
- AI-assisted Business Growth
- Transparent Operations
- Fair Marketplace Policies

---

# Seller Profile

Every Seller Account contains:

- Full Name
- Unique Username
- Mobile Number
- Profile Photo (Optional)
- NID Verification Status
- Registration Date
- Account Status

Only verified Sellers may operate Shops.

---

# Seller Dashboard

Every Seller has one Business Dashboard.

The Dashboard displays:

- My Shops
- Orders Summary
- Wallet Summary
- Reviews Summary
- Badge Status
- Notifications
- Performance Overview
- Sales Overview
- Policy Warnings

The Dashboard is the primary Business Control Center for every Seller.

---

# Seller Business Center

The Seller Business Center provides access to:

- Shop Management
- Product Management
- Inventory Management
- Order Management
- Return & Refund Requests
- Wallet
- Reports
- Business Analytics

---

# Seller Documents

Seller verification documents include:

- National ID (Required)
- Trade License (Optional)
- TIN (Future)
- Other Verification Documents

Verification History is permanently stored.

---

# General Seller Rules

- Every Seller has one unique Seller ID.
- Every Seller has one unique Username.
- Seller KYC is completed only once.
- One Seller may own unlimited Shops.
- Every Seller Account is permanently audited.

# Shop Management

Every Seller may manage all of their Shops from one Dashboard.

Available actions include:

- Create Shop
- Edit Shop Information
- Open Shop
- Close Shop
- View Shop Performance
- Manage Shop Settings

Each Shop operates independently.

---

# Vacation Mode

A Seller may temporarily pause Shop operations using Vacation Mode.

When Vacation Mode is enabled:

- Shop remains visible.
- Products remain visible.
- New Orders are not accepted.
- Customers see a Vacation notice.
- Followers receive a notification.

When Vacation Mode ends:

- Shop automatically returns to Active status.
- Followers receive a notification that the Shop is accepting orders again.

---

# Vacation Schedule

Sellers may schedule Vacation Mode in advance.

The system automatically:

- Starts Vacation Mode.
- Ends Vacation Mode.
- Sends notifications to Followers.

- Policy Compliance
- Order Completion Rate

- Visible to Admin.
- Used internally by AI.

# AI Limitations

AI may:

- Recommend Products.
- Recommend Prices.
- Detect suspicious activities.
- Suggest business improvements.
- Recommend Admin Review.

AI cannot:

- Suspend Seller Accounts.
- Block Seller Accounts.
- Delete Seller Accounts.
- Permanently Suspend Shops.
- Make final enforcement decisions.

Final authority always belongs to Admin.
