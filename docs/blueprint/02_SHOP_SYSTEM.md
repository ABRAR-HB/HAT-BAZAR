# HAT-BAZAR Blueprint

# Shop System

Version: 1.0

Status: Final Blueprint

---

# Purpose

The Shop System enables verified Sellers to create, manage and grow one or more Shops within the HAT-BAZAR marketplace.

Every Shop operates independently while remaining connected to its Seller Account.

---

# Scope

This Blueprint defines:

- Shop Creation
- Shop Ownership
- Shop Approval
- Shop Categories
- Shop Address
- Shop Status
- Shop Badge Integration
- Shop Business Rules

---

# Core Principles

The Shop System follows these principles:

- Fast Shop Creation
- Secure Marketplace
- AI-assisted Approval
- Independent Shop Management
- Fair Marketplace Operations

Every Shop must comply with HAT-BAZAR Marketplace Policies.

---

# Shop Ownership

Only Verified Sellers may create Shops.

One Verified Seller may own and manage an unlimited number of Shops.

Seller KYC is completed only once.

Creating additional Shops does not require another KYC verification.

---

# Shop Creation Requirements

Every Shop requires:

- Shop Name
- Shop Logo
- Shop Cover Photo
- Shop Category
- Shop Address
- GPS Location
- Business Description (Optional)

Incomplete Shop information cannot be submitted.

---

# Shop Name Rules

Every Shop Name must:

- Be unique within the Seller's own Shops.
- Follow Marketplace Naming Policies.
- Avoid prohibited or misleading words.
- Avoid trademark violations.

AI reviews every Shop Name before approval.

---

# Shop Logo & Cover

Sellers may upload any appropriate image as:

- Shop Logo
- Shop Cover

AI automatically checks uploaded images for:

- Adult Content
- Violence
- Hate Symbols
- Illegal Content

Suspicious images are forwarded for Admin Review.

---

# Shop Categories

A Shop may belong to multiple Categories.

Examples:

- Electronics
- Fashion
- Grocery
- Home & Living
- Agriculture

Categories help customers discover Shops more efficiently.

---

# General Shop Rules

- Every Shop receives a unique Shop ID.
- Shop creation history is permanently recorded.
- Every Shop belongs to exactly one Seller Account.

---

**End of Part 1**



# Shop Approval

Every newly created Shop is reviewed by AI before becoming available in the marketplace.

The AI reviews:

- Shop Name
- Shop Logo
- Shop Cover Photo
- Shop Categories
- Shop Address
- Marketplace Policy Compliance

If no issues are detected, the Shop is automatically approved within a very short time.

---

# AI Approval Rules

AI may:

- Approve valid Shops.
- Detect duplicate or misleading Shop Names.
- Detect prohibited images.
- Detect suspicious Shop information.
- Flag suspicious Shops for Admin Review.

AI cannot permanently Block a Shop.

Final authority always belongs to Admin.

---

# Admin Review

If AI detects suspicious information:

The Shop is sent to Admin Review.

The Admin may:

- Approve the Shop.
- Reject the Shop.
- Request additional information.
- Suspend Shop approval until investigation is completed.

Every Admin decision is permanently recorded.

---

# Shop Status

Every Shop maintains one official Status.

Supported Statuses:

- Pending Review
- Active
- Suspended
- Closed
- Blocked

Every Status change is permanently recorded.

---

# Shop Visibility

Only Active Shops are visible to Customers.

Pending Review, Suspended, Closed and Blocked Shops are hidden from public search and browsing.

---

# Shop Opening & Closing

Sellers may:

- Open an Active Shop.
- Close their own Shop temporarily.

Closed Shops:

- Are hidden from Customers.
- May be reopened later.
- Keep all historical data.

---

# Shop Suspension Policy

If a Shop violates Marketplace Policies:

- Only that Shop is Suspended.

Other Shops owned by the same Seller remain Active unless further violations are discovered.

Repeated or serious violations may result in Seller Account investigation.

---

# Shop History

The system permanently records:

- Shop Creation
- Approval History
- Status Changes
- Suspension History
- Reopening History

Shop History cannot be deleted.

# Shop Badge Integration

Every newly approved Shop automatically receives:

- Badge Level 4

The initial Badge Level follows the rules defined in:

- 12_BADGE_SYSTEM.md

Badge calculation is performed automatically by AI according to marketplace performance rules.

---

# Shop Performance

The system continuously evaluates Shop Performance.

Performance factors include:

- Order Completion
- Delivery Success
- Return Rate
- Policy Violations
- Customer Satisfaction
- Marketplace Compliance

Performance records are permanently stored.

---

# Shop Settings

Every Shop may manage:

- Shop Name
- Shop Logo
- Shop Cover Photo
- Shop Description
- Shop Categories
- Shop Address
- Business Hours
- Contact Number

Changes are automatically saved in Shop History.

---

# Shop Address Management

Every Shop Address includes:

- Division
- District
- Upazila/Thana
- Union/Area
- Detailed Address
- GPS Location

Sellers may update Shop Address.

Significant Address changes may require AI verification.

Suspicious changes may be forwarded to Admin Review.

---

# Shop Audit

Every Shop activity is permanently recorded.

Audit records include:

- Shop ID
- Seller ID
- Shop Creation Date
- Status History
- Badge History
- Address Changes
- Admin Actions
- AI Review Results

Audit records cannot be edited or deleted.

---

# Shop Business Rules

- Every Shop belongs to one Seller Account.
- One Seller may own unlimited Shops.
- Seller KYC is completed only once.
- Every Shop has its own independent settings.
- Every Shop has its own Badge Level.
- Every Shop has its own Performance History.
- Shop activities are permanently recorded.

---

# Shop Security

AI continuously monitors Shops for:

- Fraudulent activities
- Fake business information
- Policy violations
- Prohibited content

AI may flag suspicious Shops.

Final enforcement decisions always belong to Admin.

Future versions of the Shop System may introduce:

- Multi-Manager Support
- Shop Employees
- Branch Shops
- Shop Analytics Dashboard

- Custom Shop Themes

These features require approval from the Project Owner before implementation.

# AI Limitations

AI may:

- Approve valid Shops.
- Review Shop information.
- Detect suspicious activities.
- Monitor policy compliance.
- Recommend Admin Review.

AI cannot:

- Permanently Block Shops.
- Permanently Delete Shops.
- Suspend Seller Accounts.
- Make final enforcement decisions.

Final authority always belongs to Admin.

# Final Business Rules

- Only Verified Sellers may create Shops.
- One Seller may own unlimited Shops.
- Seller KYC is completed only once.
- Every Shop is independently managed.
- AI reviews every newly created Shop.
- Valid Shops are approved automatically within a short time.
- Suspicious Shops are forwarded to Admin Review.
- Only the violating Shop is suspended for normal policy violations.
- Shops may belong to multiple Categories.
- Every Shop maintains its own Address and GPS Location.
- Every newly approved Shop starts with Badge Level 4 according to the Badge System Blueprint.
- Shop data, history and audit records are permanently stored.
- Sellers cannot permanently delete Shops.
- Sellers may temporarily Close and later Reopen their Shops.
- AI assists Shop management, but final authority always belongs to Admin.
