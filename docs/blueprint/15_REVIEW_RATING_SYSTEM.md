# HAT-BAZAR Blueprint

# Review & Rating System

Version: 1.0

Status: Final Blueprint

---

# Purpose

The Review & Rating System allows verified customers to share their purchase experience after successful product delivery.

The system improves marketplace transparency, customer confidence and seller accountability while preventing fake or abusive reviews.

---

# Scope

This Blueprint defines:

- Review Eligibility
- Rating Rules
- Review Submission
- Review Editing
- Review Visibility
- Fake Review Detection
- AI Review Monitoring
- Administrative Review
- Business Rules

---

# Core Principles

The Review & Rating System follows these principles:

- Verified Purchase Only
- Fairness
- Transparency
- Customer Trust
- Abuse Prevention
- Permanent Review History

Only genuine customers may review purchased products.

---

# Review Eligibility

A customer may submit a review only when:

- The Order has been successfully delivered.
- The customer received the product.
- The review is submitted within the allowed review period.

Customers who did not successfully complete a purchase cannot submit reviews.

---

# Review Period

A Review must be submitted within:

- 7 days after successful delivery.

After 7 days:

- New Reviews cannot be submitted.

---

# Rating System

HAT-BAZAR uses a 5-Star Rating System.

Ratings include:

- 1 Star
- 2 Stars
- 3 Stars
- 4 Stars
- 5 Stars

---

# Review Types

The marketplace supports:

- Product Rating
- Shop Rating

Rider Ratings are managed separately under the Rider System.

---

# Review Submission

Every Review requires:

- Star Rating (Mandatory)

Optional:

- Written Comment

Customers may submit only one Review per completed Order.

---

# General Review Rules

- Only verified customers may submit Reviews.
- Every completed Order is eligible for only one Review.
- Reviews become publicly visible after submission.
- Reviews are permanently stored.

---

**End of Part 1**


# Review Editing

Customers may edit their own Review.

Editing is allowed only:

- Within 3 days after the Review is submitted.

After 3 days:

- Reviews become permanently locked.

---

# Review Deletion

Customers cannot delete submitted Reviews.

Admins cannot permanently delete Reviews.

If a Review violates marketplace policies, it may be hidden after administrative review while preserving the original record for audit purposes.

---

# Review Visibility

All submitted Reviews are publicly visible.

Customers may browse Reviews before purchasing a Product.

---

# Review Sorting

Reviews may be sorted by:

- Latest
- Highest Rating
- Lowest Rating

---

# Product Rating

Every Product has its own Rating Score.

The Product Rating is calculated automatically from verified customer Reviews.

Manual modification of Product Ratings is prohibited.

---

# Shop Rating

Every Shop has its own Rating Score.

Shop Ratings are calculated automatically using verified customer Reviews.

Manual modification of Shop Ratings is prohibited.

---

# Rating Calculation

The marketplace automatically calculates:

- Average Product Rating
- Average Shop Rating
- Total Number of Reviews

The calculation is updated whenever a new Review is submitted or an eligible Review is edited.

---

# Customer Restrictions

Customers may review only Products they actually purchased and successfully received.

Multiple Reviews for the same completed Order are not allowed.

Reviews cannot be transferred to another Product or Shop.

---

# Business Rules

- Ratings are calculated automatically.
- Manual Rating manipulation is prohibited.
- Review History is permanently stored.
- Rating data is used only for marketplace transparency.

Review data does not affect the HAT-BAZAR Badge System.

---

**End of Part 2**


# Fake Review Detection

HAT-BAZAR automatically monitors Reviews to protect marketplace integrity.

The system detects suspicious Review activities before they affect marketplace trust.

---

# AI Review Monitoring

AI continuously analyzes Reviews for suspicious patterns.

Examples include:

- Spam Reviews
- Duplicate Reviews
- Abusive Language
- Suspicious Review Behavior
- Unusual Review Patterns

AI may flag suspicious Reviews for further investigation.

---

# AI Limitations

AI acts only as an assistant.

AI cannot:

- Permanently delete Reviews.
- Permanently hide Reviews.
- Suspend Customer Accounts.
- Modify Ratings.

Final authority always belongs to Admin.

---

# Administrative Review

Flagged Reviews are forwarded to the Admin Review Queue.

Authorized Admins may:

- Review the flagged content.
- Hide Reviews that violate marketplace policies.
- Restore hidden Reviews after investigation.

Every administrative action must be permanently recorded.

---

# Fraud Prevention

The Review System prevents abuse by enforcing the following rules:

- Only verified customers may submit Reviews.
- Only one Review is allowed for each completed Order.
- Reviews cannot be transferred between Products or Shops.
- Suspicious activities are automatically monitored.

---

# Review Moderation

Reviews must not contain:

- Offensive Language
- Hate Speech
- Threats
- Personal Information
- Illegal Content
- Spam

Violating Reviews may be hidden after Admin Review.

---

# Audit Trail

Every Review-related administrative action is permanently recorded.

Audit information includes:

- Review ID
- Admin ID
- Action Taken
- Reason
- Date & Time

Audit records cannot be edited or deleted.

---

# Business Rules

- AI assists Review moderation.
- AI cannot make final decisions.
- Admin decisions are permanently logged.
- Customer trust is prioritized.
- Marketplace transparency must always be maintained.

---

**End of Part 3**


# Review Integrity

The Review & Rating System must always maintain fairness, transparency and customer trust.

Review manipulation in any form is strictly prohibited.

---

# Marketplace Integrity

Reviews represent genuine customer experiences.

No Seller, Customer or third party may artificially influence Ratings or Reviews.

The marketplace continuously monitors Review quality.

---

# System Limitations

The Review & Rating System does not affect:

- Badge Level
- Wallet Balance
- Escrow
- Seller Settlement
- Delivery Operations

The system is designed solely to improve marketplace transparency.

# Final Business Rules

- Only verified customers may submit Reviews.
- Reviews must be submitted within 7 days after successful delivery.
- Customers may edit Reviews within 3 days after submission.
- Reviews cannot be deleted by Customers.
- Sellers cannot reply to Reviews.
- Every completed Order allows only one Review.
- Product Rating and Shop Rating are calculated automatically.
- Review data does not affect the Badge System.
- AI may detect suspicious Reviews but cannot make final decisions.
- Flagged Reviews require Admin Review.
- Review history is permanently stored.
- Every administrative Review action is permanently audited.
